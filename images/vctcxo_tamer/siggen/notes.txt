Oscilloscope = Agilent MSO-X 3054A

Oscilloscope has signal generator. Set:
  - Square wave
  - 1.8 Vpp w/ 900 mV offset to create 0 - 1.8 V

For 1PPS:
  - 20% duty cycle, 1 Hz

For 10 MHz:
  - 50% duty cycle, 10 MHz


siggen_tune_10mhz.png
  10 MHz
  Initialization routine (course tune).
  Settles out to ~682 mV

    Loading fpga...
    Done.
    bladeRF> set vctcxo_tamer 10MHz

      VCTCXO tamer mode: 10 MHz

    Stored VCTCXO trim:  0x8b28
