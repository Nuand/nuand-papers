Very close to factory calibrated value. Differences can be attribtuted to:
  - Setup error (signal is 3.3 V 50 Ohm through 10 dB attenuator for pin on 1.8 V FPGA I/O bank)
  - Ambient conditions (temperature (~70F), air circulation, different reference oscillator)
